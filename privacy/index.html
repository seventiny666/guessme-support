Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-03-17

GuessMe is completely offline and privacy-focused. We don't collect any personal information, don't track user behavior, and all data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information. GuessMe operates completely offline and does not require any personal data to function.

  • ❌ No personal information
  • ❌ No usage analytics
  • ❌ No device identifiers
  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No contact information

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use any information. All game data (scores, custom categories, preferences) is stored locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms:

  • ✅ Game preferences stored in iOS UserDefaults
  • ✅ Custom categories stored locally
  • ✅ No data sent to external servers
  • ✅ Data is deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Third-Party Services

GuessMe uses minimal third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and subscription management
  • Apple StoreKit: For in-app purchases (handled by Apple)

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

GuessMe is safe for children of all ages. We don't collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.

6. Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, your privacy is inherently protected:

  • ✅ No network connections required
  • ✅ No data transmission
  • ✅ All data stays on your device
  • ✅ Protected by iOS security features

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your local data:

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Reset app data through iOS Settings
  • No need to request data deletion from us

8. International Users

GuessMe works the same way worldwide - completely offline with no data collection, regardless of your location.

9. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
