🎮 Privacy Policy

GuessMe

📅 Last Updated: February 28, 2026

📋 Summary

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app runs completely offline, and all data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Information Collection

"GuessMe" is a completely offline party game app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Device information
  • Location information
  • Usage data
  • Game records

2. Data Storage

All app functions run locally on your device:

  • Game data is temporarily stored in device memory only during gameplay
  • Custom category data is stored locally on the device (UserDefaults)
  • Subscription status is managed by Apple, which we cannot access
  • No data is uploaded to any server

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services (such as Google Analytics)
  • Advertising services
  • Social media integration
  • Cloud storage services

4. Network Connection

This app runs completely offline and does not require an internet connection. The app will not:

  • Access the internet
  • Send or receive any data
  • Connect to any server

Note: Subscription purchases require connection to Apple servers, but this is handled by the iOS system, and we cannot access your payment information.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages (4+). Since we do not collect any information, we do not collect personal information from children.

6. Permissions

This app does not request any system permissions, including:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Network access (except for subscription purchases)

7. Data Security

Since this app does not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data breach. All game content runs locally on your device.

8. Subscription Information

If you purchase a Pro subscription:

  • Subscriptions are handled by Apple's App Store
  • We cannot access your payment information
  • Subscription status is stored in your Apple ID
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions anytime in iOS Settings

9. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and the "Last Updated" date will be updated.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through:
