Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How to start a game?

1. Select a category on the main screen
2. Choose game duration (30s/60s/90s)
3. Tap "Start Game"
4. One person acts out while another guesses
5. Tap ✓ for correct answers, skip if needed

💎 How to subscribe to Pro?

1. Tap "Upgrade to Pro" on the main screen
2. Review subscription details
3. Tap "Start Free Trial"
4. Free for 3 days, then $1.99/year auto-renewal

🔄 How to restore purchases?

1. Open the subscription screen
2. Tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom
3. The system will automatically check and restore your subscription

❌ How to cancel subscription?

1. Open iPhone "Settings"
2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
3. Select "Subscriptions"
4. Find "GuessMe"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Does it support Family Sharing?

Yes! The subscription supports Family Sharing. After subscribing, your family members can also use Pro features for free.

🔐 Does the app collect personal information?

No! This app runs completely offline, doesn't collect any personal information, doesn't track user behavior, and all data is stored locally on your device only.

📶 Does it require internet connection?

No! The app runs completely offline and doesn't require internet connection to enjoy all features.

🆓 What's the difference between Free and Pro versions?

Free version: Access to 3 categories (Random, Idioms, Food)
Pro version: Unlock all 10 categories including General Knowledge, Slang, Animals, Literature, Internet Terms, Professions, Anime Characters
